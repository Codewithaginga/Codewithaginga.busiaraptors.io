About Us

History

Busia Raptors, Kenya professional Basketball team based in Busia County. The Raptors have won several titles among them 2019 best Team in the country for the inter-county tournament. The club starts its operation in 2006 and joined KCBL in 2018. The club consist of both male and female palyers. In doing so bridging the gap of gender in sport in the community.
